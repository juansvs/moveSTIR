GPS tracking data on wild pigs collared in 2017 in Florida. See the associated manuscript for details on the study design.

Columns
-------

- `latitude`: The latitude of a GPS fix
- `longitude`: The longitude of a GPS fix
- `date_time`: The date time of a GPS fix
- `individual_ID`: Unique identifier for collared wild pigs
- `UTMx`: The UTM Easting for the GPS fix (meters)
- `UTMy`: The UTM Northing for the GPS fix (meters)

The proj4 string for the UTM projection is: "+proj=utm +zone=17 +ellps=WGS84 +datum=WGS84 +units=m +no_defs"
